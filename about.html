Our vision

Girls Fawkes is a project aiming at having a better and sustainable food supply chain...at home! We believe that the food we have in our cupboards and fridges is precious and should not be wasted. We know how delicious and easy some recipies are and how good we can be too...if we use our leftovers to cook!

Join our mission and enjoy delicious and seaonal recipies across all the four seasons!

P.S. If you have an original or grandma's recipe you love...share it with us: We will create a webpage for it!
