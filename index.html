Sustainable Food: Leeds

This is a website aiming to reduce food waste by providing local, seaonal recipes. We are also committed to reducing our carbon footprint by eating sustainably. The recipes page will show you recipes based on food you select from the drop down. The restaurants page will show you where you can go locally to get sustainably sourced food. If you have any additional suggestions for websites and places to eat that we have missed off, please get in contact with us using our contact page.
