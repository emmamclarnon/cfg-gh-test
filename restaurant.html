Sustainable Restaurants in Leeds

Restaurants offering sustainable food in Leeds

Seven Arts

This bar is a not-for-profit, independent arts space. First set up in 2007, it hosts a range of live music and comedy, as well as Cafe Scientifique and art exhibitions. As well as being an arts space, it also serves great food and delicious beers.

LS6 Cafe

One of the oldest cafes in Leeds, LS6 has nursed many a hangover through the medium of breakfast. It sources food locally and ensures it comes from sustainable suppliers and farms. Supporting local business and a Sunday film night with an emphasis on social issues, what's not to love?
