Sustainable Restaurants in Leeds

There are a number of restaurants and cafes in Leeds offering sustainable food choices. We have listed some of our favourites and will update this list as we discover more restaurants.

Restaurants offering sustainable food in Leeds

Seven Arts

This bar is a not-for-profit, independent arts space. First set up in 2007, it hosts a range of live music and comedy, as well as Cafe Scientifique and art exhibitions. As well as being an arts space, it also serves great food and delicious beers.

LS6 Cafe

One of the oldest cafes in Leeds, LS6 has nursed many a hangover through the medium of breakfast. It sources food locally and ensures it comes from sustainable suppliers and farms. Supporting local business and a Sunday film night with an emphasis on social issues, what's not to love?

Nichols Vegetarian Delicatessen

Offering vegetarian and vegan options since 2012, this small dwelling has lots to offer those who wish to eat non-animal alternatives. Situated in Chapel Allerton, it offers a pleasant place for lunch or dinner out of the buzz of the city.

Inkwell Arts

A safe and creative art space with a focus on postive mental health experiences, this cafe is ran by volunteers and staff. It offers a selection of vegetarian and vegan food for those who are taking a class, or those who want to pop in for a coffee and sandwich.
